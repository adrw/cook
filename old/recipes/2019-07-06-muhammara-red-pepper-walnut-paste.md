# Blend

* ⅔ container fresh red peppers
* ⅓ container walnuts
* ½ cup roasted red pepper (with the jar water - don’t get ones pickled in sunflower oil)
* ¼ cup olive oil
* Couple cloves of garlic
* 1-2 tbsp roasted red pepper paste
* Salt
