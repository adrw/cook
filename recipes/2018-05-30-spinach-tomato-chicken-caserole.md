# Ingredients

- Cheese
- Cherry tomatoes (+ salt / pepper)
- Spinach (+ salt / pepper)
- Chicken thighs
- Sauce (tomato)

### Chicken Seasoning

- Basil
- Oregano
- Italian spice
- Pepper
- Red pepper flakes

# Steps

- Mix seasoning and mix with 1 inch cubed chicken
- Layer in a caserole dish
  - Sauce
  - Cheese
  - Spinach
  - Tomatoes
  - Chicken
